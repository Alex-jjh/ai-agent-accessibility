retry correlate with lower success? (If yes → the 429 added an

> ✅ No operator shows a significant retries ↔ outcome correlation (at α=0.05, two-sided). Retries absorbed without affecting outcomes.

### §2.2 Across-operator confound test (rank correlation)

If operators with more 429 retries are *also* the ones with lower success rates, that's a confound: we can't cleanly attribute the drop to the operator vs to throttling.

Spearman ρ = **-0.064**, p = **0.756** (N=26 operators)
> ✅ No significant rank correlation between retry rate and success rate.
