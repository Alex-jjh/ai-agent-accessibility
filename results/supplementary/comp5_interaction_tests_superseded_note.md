# Note: `results/stats/interaction_tests.csv` is degenerate and superseded

`results/stats/interaction_tests.csv` is a **frozen, non-converged composite
GLM**, not the C.2 composition interaction test. Its `agent_x_variant` rows
carry separation-driven coefficients (e.g. `const ≈ −7.99e22`, `se = 0.0`,
`p = 0.0`) and its `model_x_variant` rows are all NaN. It must NOT be read as a
per-pair interaction test, and per the repo's frozen-data policy it is left
**unmodified**.

For per-pair composition interaction inference, use instead:

- `results/supplementary/comp5_interaction_bootstrap_ci.csv` — per-pair
  bootstrap 95% CI on the interaction term (B=2,000, seed 20260607). Only 2/28
  pairs (L6+L11, L4+L6) have a CI excluding zero.
- `results/supplementary/comp2_gt_sensitivity.csv` — aggregate omnibus tests
  (one-sample t / Wilcoxon / sign) on the 28 interaction terms.

Paper §5.4 and `paper-supplementary/statistics-mode-a-c2.md` cite these
superseding files; `main.tex` (Reviewer-Anticipated Statistical Concerns table)
no longer cites a per-pair Fisher+Holm "confirmation" of super-additivity.
